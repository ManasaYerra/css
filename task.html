WHAT IS BIRYANI?


Biryani, also known as biriyani,
biriani, birani or briyani, is a delicious
Indian seasoned rice dish. YUM!! It can
be made with meat, fish, or vegetables.



TYPES OF BIRYANI


  • HYDERABADI BIRYANI
  • LUCKNOWI, OR AWADHI, BIRYANI
  • KOLKOTA BIRYANI
  • SINDHI BIRYANI
  • MEMONI BIRYANI
  • MALABAR BIRYANI
